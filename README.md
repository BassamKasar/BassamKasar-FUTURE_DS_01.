# Adidas Sales Performance Analytics (2020-2021)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project was completed as part of the **Data Science & Analytics Internship** at **Future Interns**. The goal was to transform raw business data from a recognizable brand (Adidas) into actionable insights using data cleaning, KPI analysis, and professional visualization.

The analysis focuses on answering key business questions:
* Which products generate the most revenue?
* How do sales trends fluctuate over a two-year period?
* Which regions and sales channels are the most profitable?

## 🛠️ Tools Used
* **Power BI**: For dashboard creation and interactive reporting.
* **Microsoft Excel**: For initial data exploration and cleaning.

## 📊 Key Insights & KPIs
Based on the **Adidas Data Sales.pbix** report and **image_1a0905.png**:

* **Total Revenue**: $899.90 Million.
* **Total Operating Profit**: $332.13 Million.
* **Best Selling Product**: **Men's Street Footwear**, surpassing $200M in sales.
* **Top Sales Channel**: **In-store** sales lead with 39.63% ($356.64M), though digital and outlet channels are close behind.
* **Seasonality**: Sales peaked significantly in **July** and saw a secondary surge in **December**.

## 📁 Repository Structure
* `Adidas US Sales Datasets.xlsx`: The raw dataset used for analysis.
* `Adidas Data Sales.pbix`: The final Power BI dashboard file.
* `Adidas_Sales_Report.pdf`: A detailed business report with strategic recommendations.

## 💡 Business Recommendations
1. **Inventory Scaling**: Increase stock levels for lifestyle footwear categories in Q2 to prepare for the July sales peak.
2. **Regional Focus**: Direct marketing efforts toward the **West Region**, as it shows the highest concentration of profitable sales.[cite: 1]
3. **Omnichannel Strategy**: Invest in "Buy Online, Pick Up In-Store" (BOPIS) to bridge the gap between the growing Online channel (27%) and the dominant In-store channel (39%).[cite: 1]
